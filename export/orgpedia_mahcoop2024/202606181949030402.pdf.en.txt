3. Letter from the Commissioner and Registrar of Cooperatives, Co-operative Societies, Pune No. Krishi Pat-15 / Loan waiver / Implementation / 2026/1178. Dated 08/06/2026.
The Cabinet of Ministers. The Punyashlok Ahilyadevi Holkar Farmers Debt Relief Scheme, 2026 has been approved in the meeting held on 02.06.2026. The government has taken a decision accordingly. As mentioned in the 02.06.2026, the eligible farmers under the scheme will be given a loan waiver of up to Rs.2 lakh and an incentive of up to Rs.50,000 for regular repayment of loans. Benefits will also be provided under one-time settlement (01'5) for eligible farmers whose outstanding amount is more than ₹2 lakh.
Since the scheme is to be implemented in a time-bound manner, the collection, presentation, processing, and distribution of loan account information is important. A District Level Committee headed by the District Collector has been constituted at the district level vide Government Decision dated 11/06/2026 to conduct the proceedings within a limited period. The committee will issue a press note regarding the publication of the list of Aadhaar-free loan accounts, the list of farmers eligible for benefits under the scheme, the registration of farmers in the sue registry, and so on.
Organizing fairs for Aadhaar authentication of farmers, giving publicity to it, redressal of grievances in the matter, giving publicity to the scheme at the district level, etc.

The Commissioner and Registrar of Co-operatives, Co-operative Societies, Pune, vide Reference No. The proposal has been submitted to the government as per the letter in letter 3. However, as budgetary provision for the scheme is not available at present, the amount is not available from the balance of funds at the level of the Office of the Commissioner of Cooperation under the Mahatma Jotirao Phule Kisan Loan Waiver Scheme, 2019. The government was considering approving an expenditure of ₹1.70 crore (₹1.70 crore only).
The Government's Decision-
For the effective implementation of Punyashlok Ahilyadevi Holkar Farmers Debt Relief Scheme, 2026, all District Sub-Registrars, Cooperative Societies (excluding Mumbai City / Mumbai Suburban) will be provided with an amount of Rs. 1 crore each from the balance fund at the level of the Office of the Commissioner of Cooperation under Mahatma Jyotirao Phule Farmers Debt Relief Scheme, 2019 for the working expenses assigned to the District Level Committee constituted under the Chairmanship of the Collector. The total amount of the fund is Rs.5.00 lakh (Rs.5 lakh only). An outlay of ₹1.70 crore (₹1.70 crore) is being approved. The amount should be spent by the District Sub-Registrar, Co-operative Societies in consultation with the District Level Committee of the concerned district.
